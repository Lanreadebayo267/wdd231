GreenPlate - Site Plan

A Nigerian Plant-Based Recipes Hub

Site Name

GreenPlate - This name was chosen because it combines the ideas of fresh, eco-friendly eating ("green") and the joy of Nigerian meals ("plate"). It reflects healthy plant-based living and Nigerian cultural flavors.

Site Purpose

The site will provide visitors with easy access to plant-based Nigerian recipes, cooking tips, and ingredient substitutions. It will serve as a hub for people seeking to eat healthy while exploring Nigerian cuisine. Visitors will also be able to submit their own recipes through a form.

Scenarios

  • "“What are some easy Nigerian vegetarian recipes I can cook at home?"
  • "How can I replace meat in popular Nigerian dishes with plant-based alternatives?”

Color Schema

Green (#2e7d32): Used for headings, accents, and navigation elements.
Sand (#f4e1c6): Used for backgrounds and secondary areas to create warmth and balance.

Typography

  • Montserrat (Sans-serif): Used for headings to give a bold, modern feel.
  • Roboto (Sans-serif): Used for body text for readability and simplicity.
